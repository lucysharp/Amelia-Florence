Amelia (Florence) Radcliffe is a self-taught artist who is currently studying Business with Strategic Economics at the university of Edinburgh. However her creativity and love of art began at a young age. She started her career in fine art after receiving much attention from her GCSE and A-Level pieces. After this Amelia realised the sales potential of her work started completing commissions for friends and family.
Amelia then undertook a partnership with 'The Vintage Tack Room' in West Sussex which broadened her business as well as giving her national recognition.
Amelia submitted a 'Scottish' collection of drawings and paintings to be included in an exhibition held at The Dundas Street Gallery in Edinburgh, which raised money for Maggie's Cancer Care.
Amelia has now diversified into producing prints and greetings cards of her work, which she also sells on request.
